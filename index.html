Welcome to a culinary experience

From the minute you step into into our restaurant you will realise this is more than just another pizza joint.

North Side Pizzas is a family owned pizzeria with one goal - to feed your stomachs with delicious pizzas made from top quality ingredients and homemade pizza dough that is produced in-house daily.

We strive to stay true to our Italian heritage.
